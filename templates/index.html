Ecotourism

Ecotourism is a type of tourism that focuses on sustainable travel to natural areas, with the goal of helping to conserve the environment and the local communities.

Examples: Kakum National Park, Mole National Park, Boabeng-Fiehma Monkey Sanctuary

Adventure

Adventure tourism refers to travel activities that involve exploration, excitement, and physical challenges in natural or cultural environments.

Examples: Kakum National Park, The Tafi Atome Monkey Sanctuary, Bui National Park

Nature

Nature tourism, also known sustainable tourism, focuses on experiencing and appreciating natural environments while minimizing negative impacts on the environment and supporting local communities.

Examples: Wli Waterfalls, Lake Bosomtwe, Aburi Botanical Gardens

Heritage

Heritage tourism is a form of travel that focuses on experiencing and appreciating the historical, and traditional aspects of a destination.

Examples: Cape Coast Castle, Elmina Castle, Larabanga Mosque

Culture

Culture refers to the shared beliefs, customs, traditions, values, language, arts, and social behaviors of a particular group of people.

Examples: Nalerigu Slave Market, Gambaga Witches Camp
